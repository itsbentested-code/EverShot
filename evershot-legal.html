Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information EverShot Cam accesses, how it is used, and what we do (and do not) do with it.

Information We Do Not Collect

EverShot Cam does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any account information.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not use any analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.
  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • No data ever leaves your device.

Device Permissions

EverShot Cam requests the following device permissions solely to provide its core functionality:

  • Camera — Required to capture video using your device's rear cameras. Without this permission the app cannot function.
  • Microphone — Required to record audio alongside your video. Without this permission video will be recorded without sound.
  • Photos (Add Only) — Required to save your finished recordings to your Photos library. We never read from your photo library — we only write to it.

You can revoke these permissions at any time in your iPhone's Settings app. Revoking a permission will disable the corresponding feature.

Data Stored on Your Device

EverShot Cam stores a small amount of data locally on your device only:

  • App preferences — Settings such as resolution, frame rate, and overlay preferences are saved in your device's local storage (UserDefaults) if you enable "Save Preferences" in the app. This data never leaves your device.
  • Teleprompter script — Any script text you type into the teleprompter is saved locally in the app's private Documents folder on your device. This data is never transmitted or shared.
  • Recorded video — Videos are written directly to your Photos library and temporary files are cleaned up after saving. We do not retain copies.

Third-Party Services

EverShot Cam does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. No third-party SDKs that collect user data are included in the app.

Children's Privacy

EverShot Cam does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is rated 4+ on the App Store.
